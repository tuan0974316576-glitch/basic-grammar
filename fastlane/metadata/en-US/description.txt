English Conqueror is an educational English learning game that combines battleship strategy with vocabulary, grammar, listening and speaking practice.

Create your commander profile, deploy your fleet, answer English questions and complete missions while building stronger language skills. The app is designed for students who want English revision to feel more interactive, focused and motivating than a traditional word list.

Features:

- Battleship-style English practice
- Reading, listening, speaking and grammar modes
- Grammar topic notes and question banks
- DSE-focused English vocabulary and grammar practice
- Fleet deployment, attack sequences and battle results
- XP, levels, supplies and unlockable progress
- Wrong-answer records and review tools
- Guest play, Google sign-in and Apple sign-in

English Conqueror turns revision into a mission. Students practise through repeated questions, quick decisions and clear progress, helping them build confidence in English step by step.

This app does not contain gambling, real-money rewards or third-party advertising. Some features may require online services for account sign-in, cloud progress saving and optional speaking practice.
