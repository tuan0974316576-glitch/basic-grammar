# Privacy Policy for Vocab Conqueror

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Vocab Conqueror is an educational English vocabulary game provided by Enguistics / Edtech Pro, LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when students, teachers, and other users play the game.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect and store:

- Account information used for sign-in, such as Google, Apple, or Firebase authentication identifiers.
- Basic profile information, such as a player callsign or display name.
- Game progress, XP, rankings, unlocked content, supplies, and gameplay records.
- Device/session identifiers used to keep the correct account connected to the correct device.
- Technical information needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve the app.
- Voice or microphone input only when the user chooses to use speaking or listening features.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Let users sign in and access their game account.
- Save learning progress and gameplay results.
- Provide classroom or student access where applicable.
- Maintain security, prevent misuse, and troubleshoot technical problems.
- Improve the educational experience.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may use service providers such as Firebase, Google, Apple, and speech/audio service providers to operate authentication, cloud storage, analytics, or learning features. These providers process information only as needed to provide their services.

## Children and Students

Vocab Conqueror may be used in educational settings. If a user is under the age required to consent to data processing in their location, the app should be used with permission from a parent, guardian, school, or teacher.

## Data Retention and Deletion

We keep account and progress information while it is needed to provide the app. Users, parents, guardians, or schools may request deletion of account data through the developer contact information shown on the app store listing.

To request deletion of a Vocab Conqueror account and associated data:

1. Contact the developer using the developer contact email shown on the Google Play or App Store listing.
2. Include the subject line "Vocab Conqueror Account Deletion Request".
3. Include the player callsign, sign-in method used, and any email address connected to the account.
4. We will delete or anonymize the account profile, authentication-linked app record, gameplay progress, XP, rankings, supplies, session identifiers, and related learning records where technically possible.
5. Some limited records may be retained only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or platform reporting obligations.
6. Deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days after we have enough information to identify the account.

## Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user information. No online service can guarantee complete security.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at the same policy URL with a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, please use the developer contact information shown on the Google Play or App Store listing for Vocab Conqueror.
