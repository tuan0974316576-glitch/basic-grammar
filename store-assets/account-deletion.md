# Vocab Conqueror Account and Data Deletion

Effective date: May 15, 2026

This page explains how users can request deletion of a Vocab Conqueror account and associated app data. Vocab Conqueror is provided by Enguistics / Edtech Pro, LLC.

## How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Vocab Conqueror account:

1. Contact the developer using the developer contact email shown on the Google Play or App Store listing for Vocab Conqueror.
2. Use the subject line: "Vocab Conqueror Account Deletion Request".
3. Include your player callsign, sign-in method used, and any email address connected to the account.
4. If the account belongs to a student, a parent, guardian, school, or teacher may submit the request.

## Data Deleted

When an account deletion request is verified, we will delete or anonymize the following data where technically possible:

- Account profile and display name/callsign.
- Firebase authentication-linked app record.
- Game progress, XP, rankings, unlocked content, supplies, and gameplay records.
- Device/session identifiers linked to the account.
- Learning progress and practice records.
- Voice or speaking practice records, if any were stored.

## Data That May Be Retained

Some limited records may be retained only where required for:

- Security and fraud prevention.
- Legal compliance.
- Dispute handling.
- Platform reporting obligations.
- Backup or system logs that are automatically removed according to normal retention cycles.

Deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days after we have enough information to identify the account.

## Data Deletion Without Deleting the Account

Users may also request deletion of selected learning or gameplay data without deleting the entire account by contacting the developer using the same contact method and clearly describing the data they want deleted.
