# Google Play Store Listing

## App Name

Vocab Conqueror

## Short Description

Learn English vocabulary through tactical battles and mission-based practice.

## Full Description

Vocab Conqueror is an educational English vocabulary game that turns word learning into a tactical battle experience.

Build your command profile, deploy your fleet, and complete mission-based challenges while reviewing English words, meanings, listening skills, grammar, and speaking practice. The app is designed for students who want vocabulary practice to feel more active and motivating than a normal word list.

Features:

- Mission-based English vocabulary practice.
- Reading, listening, speaking, and grammar modes.
- Tactical battle theme with ship deployment and attack sequences.
- Progress tracking with XP, ranks, supplies, and unlockable content.
- Vocabulary lists and review tools for repeated practice.
- Guest play, Google sign-in, and Apple sign-in support.
- Designed for classroom and independent learning use.

Vocab Conqueror is suitable for learners who enjoy game-based study and want to improve English vocabulary through repeated practice, quick recall, and interactive challenges.

The app does not contain gambling, real-money rewards, or advertising. Some features may use online services for account login, cloud progress saving, and optional speaking practice.
